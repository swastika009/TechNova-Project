We use cookies and similar technologies to make our website work properly, improve your experience, and analyse how our website is used. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the interactive panel in Section 5 below.

1What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

Similar technologies we may use include:

  • Web beacons / pixel tags: Small transparent images embedded in emails or web pages that track whether content has been opened or viewed.
  • Local storage: Data stored locally in your browser (similar to cookies but not sent to our servers automatically).
  • Session storage: Temporary data stored for the duration of your browser session.

Throughout this policy, "cookies" refers to all such technologies collectively.

2How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website functions correctly and securely;
  • Remember your cookie preferences and consent;
  • Maintain your session when using our Client Portal;
  • Understand how visitors use our website (pages visited, time spent, referral sources);
  • Improve the performance, content, and user experience of our website;
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns;
  • Deliver relevant content based on your browsing behaviour (with your consent).

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, passwords, or health data, and we do not sell cookie-derived data to third parties.

6Browser Controls

In addition to the preference panel above, you can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies;
  • Block all cookies or specific types of cookies;
  • Set alerts when cookies are being placed.

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of our website and some features may not work as intended. Guidance for major browsers:

🌐 Chrome 🦊 Firefox 🧭 Safari 🔷 Edge

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting Your Online Choices or the Network Advertising Initiative.
