FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can TechNova start on my project?

+
We typically begin discovery and kick-off within 5–10 business days of contract signing. For urgent projects, we can often accommodate a faster start. Reach out to discuss your specific timeline.

Do you work with startups as well as enterprise companies?

+
Absolutely. We work with startup founders, SMEs, and enterprise organisations across all sectors. Our approach scales to fit your budget, complexity, and stage of growth.

What is your typical project delivery model?

+
We follow an agile delivery model with 2-week sprints, regular demos, and full transparency throughout. You'll have access to a dedicated project manager and a real-time dashboard showing progress at all times.

Do you offer post-launch support?

+
Yes. All projects include a post-launch support period, and we offer ongoing retainer-based support and maintenance packages for teams who want continued optimisation, monitoring, and development.

How do you handle data security and GDPR compliance?

+
Security and compliance are built into every solution from the ground up. We follow GDPR best practices, use encryption at rest and in transit, and conduct security audits as part of our standard delivery process.

What information should I prepare for the initial consultation?

+
A brief description of your business, the problem you're trying to solve, your target users, desired timeline, and budget range is a great starting point. We'll guide you through the rest during the consultation.
