How to Apply

Our Hiring Process

1
Apply Online
Submit your CV and a short note about why you want to join TechNova. No lengthy cover letters — just your work and your story.
2
Intro Call (30 min)
A quick chat with our talent team to learn about you, your experience, and what you're looking for. We'll tell you more about the role and TechNova.
3
Technical Interview
A practical, real-world task relevant to the role — no puzzles or trick questions. We want to see how you think and solve problems.
4
Final Interview
Meet the team you'd be joining — a collaborative conversation about values, working style, and long-term goals.
5
Offer & Onboarding
We move fast. If we're aligned, you'll have an offer within 5 business days, followed by a structured onboarding programme.
